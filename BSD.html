Osamu Dazai

Osamu Dazai is a member of the Armed Detective Agency and former executive of the underworld organization,
Port Mafia. He has the Ability named No Longer Human.

Chuuya Nakahara

Chuuya Nakahara is a member of the Port Mafia as well as Dazai's former partner in Twin Dark. He is one of the five Executives in the Port Mafia. He has the Ability named Upon the Tainted Sorrow.
